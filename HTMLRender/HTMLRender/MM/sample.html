32-42-27 Brake

Removal of Brake Assembly

WARNING
Depressurize the hydraulic system before beginning this procedure. Failure to do so may cause injury.

Required Tools

Tool Part Number
Torque Wrench TW-200
Brake Lifting Fixture BLF-110

Procedure

Step 1
Apply wheel chocks.
Step 2
Release hydraulic pressure.
Step 3
Disconnect hydraulic lines.
Step 4
Remove brake retaining bolts.
Step 5
Carefully remove the brake assembly.

Figure

Figure 1. Brake Assembly
NOTE
Inspect the mounting surface for corrosion before installing the replacement brake.
